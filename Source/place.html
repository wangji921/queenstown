Left Bank Cafe

from $15

Named after that hallowed haunt of artists, writers and philosophers on the Seine, Left Bank is a smart sidewalk concept café, part open French-kitchen, part Parisian tea room.

Highlights includes 1930’s – 1950’s art, plus vintage butcher’s table, window shutters and mosaic floor tiles shipped from France. Wrought iron gating, adds to Left Bank Café’s egalitarian elegance, while a simple rope tie in Kowhai yellow ceremoniously wraps across the café’s padlock each evening, symbolising the end of another day with a does of old work tradition and nostalgia.

 Hours:

  • Breakfast: 7 days a week 6:30am – 10:30am
  • Lunch: Monday to Friday 12:30pm – 3:30pm
  • Dinner: 7 days a week 5:30pm – 9:00pm

 Address: 8 Duke Street, Queenstown, 9300

Gantley's Historic Restaurant

$15 - $40

Built in 1863 during the Gold Rush, the ambiance created by our beautiful historic stone restaurant is romantic and elegant, with exposed stone walls, soft lighting, and a huge open fire-place. The tables are set with starched linen napery, glowing candles and beautiful crystal stemware.

We have 2 acres of landscaped gardens where guests can enjoy pre or after dinner drinks or simply admire the majestic mountain scenery.

At Gantley's we take pride in presenting a unique setting and unsurpassed quality as the venue for conference and incentive group cocktail parties, dinners and gala evenings as well as wedding ceremonies and receptions, with a range of menus to suit every occasion.

 Hours:

  • Open 7 Days for Dinner, Reservations Essential.

 Address: Arthurs Point Road, Queenstown

threesixty Restaurant & Bar

$15 - $80

threesixty, located on Beach Street opposite Steamer Wharf, serves stylish dishes inspired by a 'Down to Earth' philosophy.

This simple, tasty cuisine is made up by the freshest local ingredients utilising modern Europen cooking techniques. threesixty provides panoramic lake views in a contemporary restaurant and bar with several distinct environments.

The main bar and restaurant area has stunning views of Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables whilst the Bed Bar features a custom-made, oversize divan with huge throw cushions. The Martini Room is enclosed by opulent deep red velvet curtains, nestled behind huge paddle doors. threesixty provides a unique dining experience that should not be missed.

 Hours:

  • 6pm – late

 Address: Beach Street, Queenstown, 9300

Glenorchy Air

$155 - $860

Board one of our small planes and be amazed as you experience some of the most spectacular scenic flights in the world. Enjoy unimpaired views of the South Islands’ spectacular scenery, taking in the snow-capped Southern Alps, magnificent fiords, mountain lakes and glaciers.

Our aim is to share with you our beautiful environment and its unique wildlife. We operate flights to Milford Sound, Mount Cook National Park and Eco-tours to see Yellow-eyed Penguins and the Royal Albatross colonies. We also offer both air and land tours around Lord of the Rings and Hobbit location sites. For the more adventurous, this winter board a scenic flight from Queenstown to Treble Cone and ski the South Island’s largest ski resort.

Glenorchy Air is family owned and operated since 1992 and have a 100% safety record. We specialise in providing the highest standard of personal, friendly service.

 Hours:

  • 7am - 9pm seven days a week

 Address: 91 McBride Street, Queenstown, 9300

KJet High Flyer Combo

$350

Take an exciting KJet boat ride from the Main Town Pier in the centre of Queenstown. In an unforgettable jet boat experience you’ll be transported from the sophisticated downtown area into the channels of two of New Zealand’s most scenic rivers.

Across the pristine waters of Lake Wakatipu and through the tranquil beauty of the willow-lined Kawarau River, the jet boat then speeds into the shallow, fast-moving braids of the Shotover River.

On the return to town our driver will drop you off at the Willows Jetty in Frankton; from here you will be transferred to your helicopter. Enjoy a personal experience with Glacier Southern Lakes Helicopters, fly from Frankton to the Remarkables Mountain Range for a sensational photo opportunity at 5,000 feet before a scenic flight over Queenstown and landing at Bob’s Peak. The helicopter will land by the Skyline Restaurant on the summit of Bob’s Peak.

 Hours:

  • Departs everyday at 2pm.

 Address: Main Town pier Marine Parade, Queenstown, 9348

Shotover Jet

$75 - $135

World famous as the ultimate jet boat experience, Shotover Jet has thrilled over 3 million people since 1965, and now it’s your turn! Take a unique breathtaking ride through dramatic and narrow canyons, and hold on tight for Queenstown’s only exhilarating full 360° spins. ‘Can you handle the canyons?’ with award winning Shotover Jet, ‘The World’s Most Exciting Jet Boat Ride’ and the only company permitted to operate in the spectacular Shotover River Canyons.

Combining the classic New Zealand invention of the Jet Boat with the kiwi culture of adventure, Shotover Jet has become part of the fabric of what is quintessentially 'New Zealand'. People from all around the world visit Queenstown for two main reasons, scenery and adventure; Shotover Jet combines both of these in a unique, exhilarating and unforgettable way and is for many visitors the highlight of their Queenstown and New Zealand holiday.
